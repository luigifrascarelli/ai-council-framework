⊛
Where a unified CoE wins: Governance, enablement, and reporting are 100% redundant if each org runs its own. One council, one framework, per-org execution lanes saves cost and removes vendor-shopping risk.
◈
Where a federated model is needed: Demo automation (SE), agent deployment (FDE), and customer health (CS) are org-specific enough that central ownership adds friction. Federated with shared standards is the right call.
